feminine power, inspiring bravery About Our dream

Sunset Crush is a visionary beauty and retail concept founded in Latin America by Cristina Cascajares, Denisse Signorette and Fernanda Lizano in 2020. Sunset Crush's unique environment offers complete freedom of purchase and the possibility of finding classic, trendy and newly created that are always in continuous evolution. The Brands in the Sunset Crush portfolio offer a wide range of product categories such as makeup, skincare, fragrance, haircare and also have its own private label.

Today, Sunset Crush is not only the leading chain of perfumery and cosmetic stores in Latinoamerica, but also a powerful beauty presence in countries around the world.
